Should You Revisit France?

If you've already been to France, consider reducing your time here to 3 days or skipping it altogether to reallocate days to other destinations. Here are some options:

  • Increase time in Spain: Spain has many regions worth exploring, from Barcelona's architecture to Madrid's museums. An extra 1-2 days here allows for more relaxed sightseeing or side trips.
  • Increase time in Italy: Italy is full of historical treasures and beautiful countryside. Consider adding a day to explore Tuscany or Cinque Terre.
  • Add another destination: If skipping France, you could add a nearby country like Belgium (Brussels, Bruges) or Germany (Cologne, Berlin) for a quick visit.

Realistic Time in Amsterdam

3 days in Amsterdam should be enough to cover the highlights without feeling rushed:

  • Day 1: Explore the central canals, Anne Frank House, Van Gogh Museum.
  • Day 2: Day trip to Zaanse Schans (windmills) or Keukenhof Gardens (if in season).
  • Day 3: Visit Jordaan neighborhood, Vondelpark, and enjoy cycling through the city.
