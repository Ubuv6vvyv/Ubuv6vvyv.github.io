9. Create Invoice

The invoice will be opened in an Adobe PDF file. For this Adobe Reader 6.0 or higher is needed. Within the Adobe PDF file you’ll be able to print, save or email the invoice.

Tips creating invoice

Please print your invoice on your official company letterhead. This does not apply to private shippers.

Please indicate for each item in your shipment a detailed description, if known a Export Harmonised Tariff Customs Code, the quantity of the item, the unit value without VAT.

Please fill in the invoice correctly and complete, a not correct invoice might lead to delayment in Customs clearance.
